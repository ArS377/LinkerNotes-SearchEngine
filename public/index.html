About the project

Music deserves better than scattered tabs.

Liner Notes brings a recording's lyrics, credits, versions, artist context, chart history, and sources into one focused place.

The catalog currently uses a small normalized dataset while its provider integrations are built. Every fact keeps its source, and unavailable data stays honestly unavailable.
