About the project

Music deserves better than scattered tabs.

Liner Notes brings a recording's lyrics, credits, versions, artist context, chart history, and sources into one focused place.

Search spans locally enriched pages, MusicBrainz's open catalog, and Apple Music's commercial catalog. Every fact keeps its source, and unavailable data stays honestly unavailable.
